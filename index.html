About me

I design and deliver scalable, cloud-native AI systems and Lakehouse solutions that power analytics, automation, intelligent products, and enterprise decision-making.

My work spans AI-first data platforms, governance, data quality and observability, CI/CD for analytics and AI, and Generative AI systems (RAG and agent-based architectures) across AWS, Azure, Databricks, and emerging edge AI environments.
